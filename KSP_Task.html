Diwali, the festival of lights is the most popular Hindu festival all over the world. It is celebrated with great pomp and show. It is celebrated by the Hindus in all parts of the country and other countries like Fiji, Guyana, India, Malaysia, Mauritius, Myanmar, Nepal, Singapore, Sri Lanka, Suriname, and Trinidad and Tobago. Diwali is also a gazetted holiday in Nepal. It is observed in the month of October or November every year.
